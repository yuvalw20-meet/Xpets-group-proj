About us

We are a group of students from MEETx who really cares about the safety of the shelter pets? It all started when our friend told us that he went to the animal shelter for his social participation requirement and saw the way animals were treated. Right then we knew we had to do something to help the animals. So we made this website, that makes the whole process of updating more convenient, simple and fun.

Vision

To have a world where as many pets lives in a safe and comfortable environment.

Mission

To provide the most simple way to find and adopt pets without a place to stay.
